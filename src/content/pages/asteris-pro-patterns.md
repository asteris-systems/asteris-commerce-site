## Frequently asked questions

**Why a separate plugin instead of bundling everything in the free theme?**
The free Asteris theme on wp.org has ~88 patterns already — more than most premium themes. We chose to keep the free theme generous and put net-new specialty work in this paid product. Nothing was moved from free to paid. Pro Patterns is pure addition.

**Can I use these patterns commercially?**
Yes. Use them on client sites within your licence's site limit (1, 3, or 5 depending on your tier).

**What happens if I cancel?**
Patterns stay registered while you're licensed. After cancellation, the patterns stop appearing in the inserter on your sites — but any pages where you already used them keep working. Their content lives in WordPress's database like any other block content.

**Will you add more patterns?**
Yes. Regular drops — composite landing-page combos, dark-mode companions, more niche industries. All free while you're subscribed.

**Why is there no lifetime tier?**
Because we want to keep shipping updates, and lifetime kills the recurring revenue that funds that work. The founder lock gives you the price certainty you'd get from lifetime, without crippling our ability to keep building.
